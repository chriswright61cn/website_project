About Me

I have been using technology for a long, long time. I made 3D graphics in POVRAY using text files generated from Excel. I got excited when Photoshop 4 introduced layers. I used Netscape Navigator. I remember the horror of the blink tag, "website under construction" gifs and making websites work in Internet Explorer 6. On the other hand, the web is full of cats.

I have worked in a variety of jobs over many years that have included advertising, video production and graphic design. Over the years I have built a number of CMS based websites and focussed on delivering easily updatable content as fast as possible. This has meant that I have not concentrated on other aspects of web technology.

IT is always a rapidly changing sector and the tools I have used, my working methods are becoming outdated and I now need to update my skills. This includes finally learning to code properly, work collaboratively and not hack about with quick fixes to IT problems. I think the courses at Code Nation are the best way for me to achieve this.
